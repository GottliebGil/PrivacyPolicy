# Privacy Policy for Budgeo

**Last Updated: January 1, 2025**

## Introduction

Budgeo ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Budgeo, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Email Address**: Collected during sign-in to identify your account and enable authentication.
- **Display Name**: Optional name you provide for your profile.

### Financial Data
- **Transactions**: Expense records you create, including amounts, dates, descriptions, and categories.
- **Categories**: Custom categories you create for organizing expenses.
- **Budget Information**: Monthly budget settings and preferences.

### Household Data
If you choose to use the household sharing feature:
- **Household Membership**: Information about which household you belong to.
- **Shared Transactions**: Expenses you choose to share with household members.
- **Balance Information**: Settlement records between household members.

### Technical Data
- **Device Information**: Device type and operating system version for app compatibility.
- **Usage Data**: App interaction data to improve functionality.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Authenticate your identity and secure your account
- Provide core app functionality (expense tracking, budgeting, household sharing)
- Sync your data across devices
- Calculate balances and settlements within households
- Improve and optimize the app experience

## Third-Party Services

### Authentication (Auth0)
We use Auth0 for secure authentication. When you sign in, Auth0 processes your credentials. Please review [Auth0's Privacy Policy](https://auth0.com/privacy) for details on their data practices.

### Data Storage
Your data is stored securely on cloud servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Your data may be shared only in the following circumstances:
- **Household Members**: If you join a household, members can see shared transactions and balance information.
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or to protect our rights.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure authentication via Auth0
- Access controls and authentication requirements

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data associated with your account within the app.
- **Correction**: Update your profile information at any time.
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and associated data through the app settings.
- **Data Portability**: Request a copy of your data in a portable format.
- **Restrict Processing**: Request that we limit how we use your data.
- **Object to Processing**: Object to our processing of your personal data.
- **Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw your consent at any time where we rely on consent to process your data.
- **Lodge a Complaint**: If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

To exercise these rights, use the in-app settings or contact us directly.

## Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be immediately and permanently removed from our systems, except where retention is required by law.

## Children's Privacy

Budgeo is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.
